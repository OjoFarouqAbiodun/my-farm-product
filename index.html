FAQs

Got Questions? We've got fresh answers!

What is My Farm Product?

My Farm Product is an online marketplace that connects you directly with local farmers and producers, delivering fresh, high-quality products to your doorstep.

How does My Farm Product work?

Browse our platform, select the farm products you want, place your order, and enjoy quick delivery straight from the source.

How do I place an order & Is there a minimum order requirement?

Simply create an account, browse the marketplace, add products to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

There is no minimum order requirement, allowing you to shop as per your needs.

What are the delivery options?

We offer standard, express, and scheduled deliveries. Delivery availability may vary depending on your location.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is placed, you'll receive tracking information via email or SMS.

How do I become a seller on My Farm Product?

Click on the “Become a Seller” link on our homepage and fill out the registration form.

Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.

Connecting farmers directly with the marketplace for better accessibility, fair pricing, and a sustainable supply chain.
